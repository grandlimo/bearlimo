    ## Frequently Asked Questions


    **How far in advance should I book?**

    We recommend booking airport limo service 24–48 hours in advance and reserving wedding or event transportation several weeks or months ahead, especially during peak seasons. Same‑day rides may be available depending on fleet and schedule, so contact dispatch for urgent requests.


    **What do I need to reserve a ride?**

    Provide your pickup date and time, locations, flight number (if applicable), passenger count, and any special requests such as child seats, extra luggage, or multiple stops. A reservation specialist will confirm pricing, estimated travel time, and itinerary details before finalizing your booking.


    **Do you offer flat‑rate pricing?**

    Yes, we use flat‑rate pricing for most airport runs and point‑to‑point transfers, with rates confirmed at booking. Additional time, extra stops, or special requests are clearly itemized so there are no surprises on your invoice.


    **What happens if my flight is delayed?**

    Our team monitors flight status and adjusts pickup times for delays or early arrivals, helping minimize wait‑time issues and rescheduling stress. You’ll receive updated driver information if your schedule changes significantly.
